Why Trust the Bible?

Can we still trust the Bible today?

Every day we hear many voices — social media, opinions, trends, and fears. Some people wonder if the Bible is too old, changed, or no longer important. But in a noisy world, knowing what truth to trust matters more than ever.

The Bible has guided lives for generations because it points people to hope, wisdom, correction, and God’s love. Times change. Cultures change. Technology changes. But truth still matters.

“Your word is a lamp to guide my feet and a light for my path.”
Psalm 119:105

Common Misunderstanding

Some people believe the Bible is outdated or only for the past. But truth does not expire simply because the world changes.

Simple Reflection

What voice shapes your thinking most today — trends, fear, opinions, or God’s Word?

The Bible is not just an old book. It is a light for the path, a guide for the heart, and a foundation for faith.
