Sabrina’s Bajan Fruit Adventure

ABC Coloring & Early Hand Signs (Deaf Roots of Barbados)

This educational children’s series explores culture, history, and early communication through gentle stories and interactive activities.

Each book introduces young readers to island life and shared traditions. By highlighting the power of hands, expressions, and community, children (ages 3–6) learn to appreciate identity and belonging from an early age—all while discovering the vibrant fruits of Barbados from A to Z alongside the **ASL Alphabet**.
