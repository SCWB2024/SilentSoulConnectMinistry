Who We Are

Silent SoulConnect Ministry is a faith-centered space created to encourage hearts through simple, clear, and meaningful spiritual connection. We welcome Deaf, hard of hearing, and anyone seeking peace, truth, and hope in God.

🌍 Under SCWB Global Impact

Silent SoulConnect Ministry is supported by a wider vision of faith, accessibility, advocacy, and community encouragement— helping create more inclusive and meaningful spiritual spaces.

Founder Profile • Deaf Advocate • Evangelist

Sandra White-Belgrave

Sandra White-Belgrave founded Silent SoulConnect Ministry with a heart to make faith more accessible, more visual, and more welcoming for those often overlooked in spiritual spaces.

Her vision is rooted in compassion, clarity, and a desire to help people reconnect with God in a way that feels simple, steady, and real.

Where We’re Going

The Future of Silent SoulConnect

Silent SoulConnect Ministry is growing into a space where faith meets accessibility in deeper ways.

We are building pathways that make spiritual learning more visual, more inclusive, and easier to understand—especially for the Deaf and hard of hearing.

Through digital tools, guided study, and quiet reflection, we aim to reach hearts beyond physical walls and nurture a global community rooted in truth, clarity, and connection with God.
