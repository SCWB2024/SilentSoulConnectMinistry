Part 1: The Silent Shadow

A Journey into Luke 15:8

Close your eyes for a moment. Imagine a silver coin—bright, shiny, and full of value. Suddenly, it slips. It doesn't make a sound. It falls into the thick dust under a heavy wooden table.

It is now in the dark. It cannot call out for help. It cannot move itself back to the light. It sits in a silent shadow, covered by the dirt of the world.

Heart Reflection

Sometimes, life makes us feel like that coin. Have you ever felt:

  • Hidden away or ignored by others?
  • Like you have no "voice" to tell people you are hurting?
  • Like the "dust" of past mistakes has covered your shine?
The Holiness Truth: Even in the dark, the coin never lost its value. The dirt changed its look, but it did not change its worth to the Owner.
