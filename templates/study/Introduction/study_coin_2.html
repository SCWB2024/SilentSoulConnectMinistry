Part 2: The Sweeping Search

A Journey into Luke 15:8 — "Light the lamp and sweep the house."

The Owner does not leave the coin in the dark. She doesn't say, "It's just one coin, let it go." No—she takes Action.

💡

The Light

She lights a lamp. The golden flame pierces the shadows. The dark has nowhere to hide.

🧹

The Movement

She sweeps the floor. Every bit of dust is moved. She is looking with her eyes, searching for a spark of silver.

God is that Owner. He doesn't wait for you to find your way to Him. He brings the Light of the Gospel to your corner. He "sweeps" away the confusion and the noise of the world so He can see your face.

🤲 Visual Prayer

In the silence, God is moving. He is working to find you, even when you cannot see Him yet.

"Lord, thank You for not leaving me in the dark. Thank You for searching for me when I felt lost. Sweep away my fears and let Your Light shine on my heart today. Amen."
