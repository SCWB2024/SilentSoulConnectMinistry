Part 3: The Visual Victory

"Rejoice with me, for I have found the coin which I lost!" — Luke 15:9

A Flash of Silver

The broom moves one last time. Suddenly, a tiny spark catches the light of the lamp. The Owner reaches down into the dust. Her fingers close around the silver.

She doesn't just put it back in her pocket and move on. She is overwhelmed with Joy. She wipes away the dirt until the coin shines like new. She holds it up for all to see.

The Heaven Celebration

Jesus tells us that when one person is "found" by God, the angels in Heaven rejoice. In the Deaf community, we know that Visual Joy is the most powerful. Imagine the angels signing "Hallelujah" with their whole beings!

🤝

Restoration

Purpose
