Lesson 1: What Is the Bible?

Start Here — Understanding the Bible

Lesson 1 of 3

🎬 Real Life

A manual tells you how something works.

Without it, you guess—and mistakes happen.

❓ Main Question

What is the Bible, and why do we need it?

📖 Bible

2 Timothy 3:16

“All Scripture is God-breathed…”

🔍 Discover

What does this verse show?

The Bible comes from God, not just people.

This means it is not only human ideas.

God guided people to write His truth.

Example: Like a teacher helping a student write the correct answer.

Why is that important?

God knows everything and does not make mistakes.

If the Bible comes from God, it is true and reliable.

We do not have to guess what is right or wrong.

Example: Like using correct directions instead of guessing your way.

What is the Bible for?

The Bible teaches us how to live.

It shows right from wrong.

It corrects us and helps us grow.

Example: Like a guide that keeps you on the right path.

💡 Simple Truth

The Bible is God’s message to you.

It is a guide for your life.

When you follow it, you learn how to live and stay close to God.

❤️ Response

  • I TRUST God’s Word
  • I WANT to learn
  • I FOLLOW His truth

🔁 Recap

  • The Bible comes from God
  • It teaches me how to live
  • I can trust it every day

🙏 Prayer

Lord, thank You for Your Word. Help me understand and follow it daily. In Jesus’ name, Amen.
