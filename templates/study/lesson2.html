Lesson 2: Why Can I Trust the Bible?

Start Here — Understanding the Bible

Lesson 2 of 3

🎬 Real Life

When directions are wrong, you get lost.

Good directions lead you safely.

❓ Main Question

How do I know the Bible is true?

📖 Bible

Isaiah 40:8

“The word of our God stands forever.”

🔍 Discover

What does this verse show?

God’s Word does not change.

People change, but God does not.

What He says stays true forever.

Example: Like a strong rock that does not move.

Why does that matter?

If something keeps changing, you cannot trust it.

But if it stays the same, you can depend on it.

God’s Word is stable and sure.

Example: Like a clock that always shows the correct time.

What does this mean for me?

I can trust the Bible even when I feel unsure.

I do not need to depend only on feelings.

God’s truth will guide me.

Example: When life feels confusing, the Bible gives direction.

💡 Simple Truth

God’s Word is always true.

It does not change with time or people.

Because of this, you can trust it every day.

❤️ Response

  • I TRUST the Bible
  • I DO NOT depend on feelings
  • I STAND on truth

🔁 Recap

  • God’s Word does not change
  • It is always true
  • I can depend on it

🙏 Prayer

Lord, help me trust Your Word fully. Keep me grounded in truth. In Jesus’ name, Amen.
