Lesson 3: How Do I Study the Bible?

🎬 Real Life

If you rush instructions, you miss steps.

Taking time helps you understand clearly.

❓ Main Question

How can I understand the Bible correctly?

📖 Bible

Psalm 119:105

“Your word is a lamp to my feet…”

🔍 Discover

What does this verse show?

God’s Word gives direction.

It helps you see what to do next.

It guides you step by step.

Example: Like a light in a dark place.

How do I study the Bible?

Read slowly, not fast.

Ask questions about what you read.

Think about what it means.

Example: Like learning step by step, not rushing.

What must I do daily?

Spend time with God’s Word every day.

Even a little time helps you grow.

Practice what you learn.

Example: Like daily habits that make you stronger.

💡 Simple Truth

God’s Word guides your life step by step.

You understand more when you take your time.

Daily reading helps you grow stronger.

❤️ Response

  • I READ the Bible daily
  • I ASK questions
  • I APPLY what I learn

🔁 Recap

  • God’s Word gives direction
  • I study it step by step
  • I live what I learn

🙏 Prayer

Lord, teach me how to understand Your Word. Guide me daily. In Jesus’ name, Amen.
