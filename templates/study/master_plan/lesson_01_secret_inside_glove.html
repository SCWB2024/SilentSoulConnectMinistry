🔑 Memory Verse

"God said: Let Us make human person in Our image..."

Genesis 1:26

📜 The Real Fact

Think about a glove. It has the shape of a hand. It has five fingers. It looks like a hand. But a glove is empty. It cannot move. It cannot work.

A glove was made for one reason: to contain a hand. Without the hand, the glove has no meaning.

💡 The Secret Meaning

You are like that glove. God made you with His "Image." You have a mind to think and a heart to feel because God has a mind and a heart.

"A glove is a vessel for a hand. A human is a vessel for God."

This is why money, education, and partying cannot satisfy you. A glove is not happy if you fill it with sand. It is only happy when the hand is inside. You are only satisfied when God is inside your spirit.

🤔 Deep Reflection

❓

Have you been trying to fill your "vessel" with things that don't fit?

🤟

Do you realize that your "Secret Meaning" is to hold God's Life?

Conclusion: Surrender

God is the Hand. You are the Glove. Will you let Him in today?

🙏 Closing Prayer

"Lord Jesus, I am a sinner. I need You. I see now that I am a vessel made for You. Come into my spirit. Take away my sin. Fill me with Your life. I receive You as my Savior. In Jesus' name, Amen."
