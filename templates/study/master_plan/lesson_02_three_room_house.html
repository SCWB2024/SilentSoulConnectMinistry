🏛️ The Real Fact: The Tabernacle

In ancient history, God gave the people a blueprint for a building called the Tabernacle. It was a house for God to live among people. It was not just one big room; it was divided into three distinct parts.

1. Outer Court

The public area everyone could see.

2. Holy Place

The inner room for service and light.

3. Holy of Holies

The Secret Room where God lived.

💡 The Secret Meaning: YOU

The Bible says you are God's temple. Just like that building, you have three "rooms" designed for different purposes:

  • 💪
    The Body (Outer Court): This is your physical skin and bones. It touches the world through seeing, smelling, and feeling.
  • 🧠
    The Soul (Holy Place): This is your mind, your emotions, and your choices. This is the "YOU" that thinks and feels.
  • ✨
    The Spirit (Holy of Holies): This is your deepest room. It is the only part of you that can touch God.

"If you are thirsty, you give water to your Body. If you are lonely, you give friendship to your Soul. But if you feel empty inside, your Spirit is hungry for God."

🤔 Heart Check

❓

Have you been trying to fix a Spirit problem (emptiness) by only feeding your Body (money/food)?

👁️‍🗨️

Can you see that God wants to live in your "Secret Room," not just your thoughts?

🙏 Closing Prayer

"Lord Jesus, thank You for showing me how I am made. I open the door to my spirit—my Secret Room. I don't want to just know about You in my mind; I want to know You in my spirit. Come and live in me today. In Jesus' name, Amen."
