🔑 Memory Verse

"But your sins have separated you from your God..."

Isaiah 59:2

📡 The Real Fact: The Receiver

In history, when the first Radio and TV were invented, people were amazed. Invisible waves were in the air, but you needed a Receiver to see the picture.

If a TV falls and the wires inside break, the invisible waves are still there! The station is still sending the message. But the TV only shows static. It is broken inside.

{# ✅ Put a real image path here. Example: /static/img/study/broken_tv.jpg #}

Even when the screen is broken, the signal is still there.

📉 The Fall: The Damaged Spirit

God made your spirit to be a "Receiver" for His life. But when Adam sinned (Genesis 3), it was like the human "TV" fell off the table.

The Static

Sin creates "noise" in our heart. We feel guilt, fear, and loneliness. We can't see God clearly anymore.

Dead Battery

Our spirit lost its power. We are alive in our body, but "dead" to the spiritual world.

"A broken TV cannot fix its own wires. A flashlight cannot give itself new batteries. A human cannot wash away their own sin-static. We need the Maker to repair us."

🤔 Deep Question

"Have you noticed that no matter how much 'good' you do, the fuzzy feeling of being lost inside never goes away?"

This is because your 'Receiver' needs a Savior, not just a new decoration.

🙏 Closing Prayer

"Lord Jesus, I see that I am broken inside. I confess that my sin has caused static between You and me. I cannot fix myself. I ask You to come and repair my spirit. Wash away the sin and turn the light back on in my heart. In Jesus' name, Amen."
