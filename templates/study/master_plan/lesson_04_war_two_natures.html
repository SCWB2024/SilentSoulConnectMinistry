🔑 Memory Verse

"I want to do good, but evil is right there with me."

Romans 7:21

⚔️ The Real Fact: The Civil War

History is full of stories about "Civil Wars." This is a war that happens inside one country. Two kings want the same throne. Families are divided. It is the most painful kind of war because the enemy is "one of us."

In the same way, every person who wants to follow God discovers a Civil War inside their own heart. You have Two Natures fighting for control of your life.

🌱 The Secret of the Two Seeds

Why is it so hard to be good? It is because you have two different "seeds" growing in the same garden:

The Old Nature (Flesh)

This comes from Adam. It is selfish, angry, and likes to sin. It is like a heavy weight pulling you down to the dark.

The New Nature (Spirit)

This comes from God. It loves the light, wants to pray, and desires to be holy. It is like wings lifting you up.

"Think of two dogs fighting. A Black Dog (Evil) and a White Dog (Good). Which dog wins the fight? The one you feed."

🙏 Conclusion: The Final Surrender

"Lord Jesus, I feel the war inside me. I confess that I often feed my old nature. I am tired of the struggle. I choose today to feed my spirit. I give You the throne of my heart. Be the King who wins the war in me. In Jesus' name, Amen."
