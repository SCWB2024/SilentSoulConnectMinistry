Series 1 — Living Clean in a Messy World

A simple, Deaf-friendly study you can sign through slowly. Everything is in this one page.

Theme Verse

Psalm 32:5 — “I confessed all my sins to You… and You forgave me.”

Big Idea

Jesus doesn’t just clean the mess around us—He starts by cleaning the mess inside us and then teaches us how to live clean daily.

Outline (use as sections while signing)

  1. See the Mess — Honest with God (Psalm 139:23–24)
  2. Receive Grace — Not by works (Ephesians 2:8–9)
  3. New Heart — New creation (2 Corinthians 5:17)
  4. Daily Walk — Put off / put on (Ephesians 4:22–24)
  5. Shine — Light in darkness (Matthew 5:14–16)

Short Points to Sign

  • God’s love is steady; my habits can change.
  • Small steps every day beat big promises once.
  • Confess fast; forgive quick; move forward.

Reflection Prompts

  • What is one “inside mess” I will give to Jesus today?
  • What clean habit will I start (5 minutes only)?

Closing Prayer

“Lord, You know my heart. Wash me, renew me, and help me walk clean today. Amen.”
