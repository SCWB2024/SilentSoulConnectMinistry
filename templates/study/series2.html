Series 2 — From Death to Life

Simple, Deaf-friendly flow. Keep eye contact, sign slowly, pause often.

Theme Verse

Ephesians 2:8–9 — “By grace you have been saved through faith… not by works.”

Big Idea

We don’t climb up to God; Jesus comes down to us. Salvation is a gift. We receive it, then live it.

Outline

  1. Dead → Alive (Eph 2:1–5) — God’s mercy makes us alive.
  2. Grace → Gift (Eph 2:8–9) — No boasting, only gratitude.
  3. Workmanship (Eph 2:10) — Saved for good works, not by them.
  4. New Identity (2 Cor 5:17) — New creation, new path.
  5. Walk It Out (Col 2:6) — Continue as you began: by faith.

Short Points

  • Grace starts the story; grace sustains the journey.
  • Good works are fruit, not the root.
  • Gratitude fuels obedience.

Reflection Prompts

  • Where am I still trying to “earn” love from God?
  • What one good work this week will flow from gratitude?

Closing Prayer

“Jesus, thank You for saving me by grace. Teach me to walk in Your gift every day. Amen.”
