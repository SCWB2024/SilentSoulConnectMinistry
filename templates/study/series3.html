Series 3 — One in Christ

The Gospel removes walls — culture, class, disability, age. We belong together.

Theme Verse

Ephesians 2:14 — “He Himself is our peace… has broken down the dividing wall.”

Big Idea

Jesus makes a new family. Unity is not sameness; it’s love in difference.

Outline

  1. Wall Broken (Eph 2:14–16) — Peace with God, peace with people.
  2. Access to the Father (Eph 2:18) — Same Spirit, same Lord.
  3. Citizens & Family (Eph 2:19) — We belong.
  4. Living Temple (Eph 2:21–22) — God’s presence among us.
  5. Practice Unity (Eph 4:2–3) — Humility, gentleness, patience, love.

Short Points

  • Unity is a gift to guard, not a trophy to win.
  • Honor people who differ from you.
  • Choose peace over pride.

Reflection Prompts

  • Who needs my apology or my welcome today?
  • How can our church include Deaf/HoH friends better this month?

Closing Prayer

“Lord, make me a peacemaker. Build us together in love and truth. Amen.”
