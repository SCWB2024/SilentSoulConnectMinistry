Series 4 — Walking Worthy

New heart → new habits. We put off the old and put on the new.

Theme Verse

Ephesians 4:22–24 — “Put off the old… be renewed… put on the new self.”

Big Idea

Transformation is daily. The Spirit changes how we think, speak, and act.

Outline

  1. Renew the Mind (Rom 12:2) — Let truth reshape thinking.
  2. New Speech (Eph 4:29) — Words that build, not break.
  3. New Self-Control (Gal 5:22–23) — Fruit of the Spirit.
  4. New Love (Eph 5:1–2) — Imitate Christ, walk in love.
  5. New Mission (Matt 5:16) — Shine so God gets glory.

Short Points

  • Small, repeatable habits > big, rare changes.
  • Confess fast; reset fast; keep walking.
  • Ask the Spirit for help every morning.

Reflection Prompts

  • One sentence I will stop saying; one I will start saying.
  • One habit I will practice for 7 days (5 minutes only).

Closing Prayer

“Holy Spirit, renew my mind and form Christ in me. Make me a light today. Amen.”
